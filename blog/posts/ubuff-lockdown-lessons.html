UBuff Lockdown – AD Defense

April 12, 2025

Leading the Indiana Tech Cyber Warriors at Lockdown was a whole different beast. This wasn’t just about securing a few boxes—it was about leading a team under pressure, keeping morale up, and grinding through non-stop red team pressure.

Our AD server was the main battlefield. Red Team loaded us up with persistence—TacticalRMM, MeshAgent, shady scheduled tasks. I cleaned it all out manually while still guiding teammates on firewall configs, user auditing, and making sure the basics stayed online.

Throughout the comp I kept the team focused. I made sure no one spiraled, even when red got aggressive. That’s discipline.

One of my favorite moments? Seeing a note from Red Team admitting they couldn’t break back in after I locked it down. That’s how you know your defense held. Console access stayed live. DNS resolved. AD stayed up. That was our win.

This comp proved one thing—PowerShell is king, teamwork under pressure matters more than tools, and a good SOP beats panic every time. I'm proud of how we handled it. Cyber Warriors showed up.
