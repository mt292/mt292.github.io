Red Team vs Blue Team

In Lockdown v18, the red team pushed persistent shells and DNS tunnels hard. The blue team held on with GPO lockdowns and constant service restarts. This post breaks down what worked, what failed, and what I’d do differently if I ran it again.

We tracked lateral movement via WMI, blocked scheduled tasks, and reversed dropped payloads. I’ll walk through the timeline, the tools used, and the big takeaways.
