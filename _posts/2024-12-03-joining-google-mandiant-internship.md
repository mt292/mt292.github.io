## Big News!

I'm thrilled to share that I'll be joining **Google** this summer as a **Security Consultant Intern** in Chicago!

Specifically, I'll be working with **Google Mandiant** (part of Google Cloud), one of the world's leading cybersecurity consulting teams.

## About Google Mandiant

For those unfamiliar, [Mandiant](https://www.mandiant.com/) is a cybersecurity powerhouse that was acquired by Google in 2022. They're known for:

- **Incident Response**: Responding to the world's most sophisticated cyber attacks
- **Threat Intelligence**: Tracking nation-state actors and advanced persistent threats (APTs)
- **Red Team Operations**: Conducting offensive security assessments for Fortune 500 companies
- **Security Consulting**: Helping organizations improve their security posture

Mandiant has been involved in investigating some of the most high-profile breaches in history, and their threat intelligence is used by organizations worldwide.
